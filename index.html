Our Story

Marka Trading and Industry Company Limited is a Tanzania-Italy based agro-food export company with over 7 years of experience. We connect African agricultural excellence with international markets, ensuring certified quality, sustainable sourcing, and reliable logistics for global importers.

  • Transparency and long-term trust with every business partner.
  • Compliance with ISO, phytosanitary, fumigation, and export standards.
  • Built on years of consistent cooperation in Tanzania and Italy.
  • Strong local roots—operating with global standards.
Watch Video

Vision

To be the bridge of excellence connecting Tanzania's natural richness to global markets, ensuring every product tells a story of quality, sustainability, and international cooperation.

Mission

To cultivate, select, and export premium sisal fibers, cashew nuts, and spices, managing the full supply chain from land to shipment with rigorous phytosanitary standards.

Integrity & Trust

We uphold absolute transparency and build long-term trust with every business partner, fostering reliable and enduring international relationships.

Certified Quality

Strict adherence to international standards including ISO, Phytosanitary, Fumigation, and Export compliance ensures premium product quality.

Glocal Approach

Strong local roots in Tanzania combined with global operational standards guarantee sustainable and professional business practices.

Responsive & Multilingual

Our website is fully responsive across all devices and supports multiple languages: English, Italian, and Arabic for international accessibility.
